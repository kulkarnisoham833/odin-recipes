How to Cook: Pancakes



A picture of delicious pancakes.

This recipe is one of the greatest recipes of pancakes ever seen. Allrecipes is really smart.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • White sugar
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Combine buttermilk, egg, butter, and 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract in a separate bowl. Beat to break up the egg. Pour over the flour mixture; stir until just combined. Stir 1/4 cup sprinkles gently into the batter. Allow batter to rest for 5 minutes.
  3. Whisk confectioners' sugar, heavy cream, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and salt together in a small bowl until smooth. Add 2 tablespoons sprinkles.
